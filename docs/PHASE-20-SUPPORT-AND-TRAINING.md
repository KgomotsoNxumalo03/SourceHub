# Phase 20 Support and Training Process

Before inviting participants, the business owner must fill in the named support owner, backup contact, hours, channel, response targets, security escalation owner, and rollback owner.

Use `/onboarding/pilot`, the optional dashboard tour, the persona matrix, and guided scenarios. Training completion is onboarding readiness only, not a productivity measure and never a basis for idle-time, screenshot, website, or application surveillance.

Support flow:

1. Ask for workflow, approximate time, route, safe correlation ID, and impact. Never request credentials or customer exports.
2. Use `/feedback` for product observations and the operations dashboard for incidents and defects.
3. Escalate security, authorization, tenant isolation, data loss, or financial-integrity concerns immediately.
4. Pause the pilot through the protected status control when the approved emergency condition is met.
5. Preserve evidence, use incident templates, and record decision and rollback conditions.

No external customer message, support mailbox, or third-party integration is created automatically.
